Frequently Asked Questions

How soon will coins be added to my account?
Coins are typically added to your account instantly after payment confirmation. For cryptocurrency payments, it may take 1-3 network confirmations before coins are credited.
Can I get a refund for unused coins?
We don't offer refunds for purchased coins. All sales are final, but coins never expire in your account.
Are there any discounts for bulk purchases?
Yes! The larger coin packages automatically include volume discounts, giving you more value for your money.
Is my payment information secure?
Absolutely. We use industry-standard encryption and secure payment processors. We never store your full credit card details on our servers.
